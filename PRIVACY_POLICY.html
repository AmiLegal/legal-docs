Privacy Policy — Am I Legal?

Effective date: May 28, 2026 App name: Am I Legal? Developer: Grow Shop Network LLC Contact: growshopnetwork@gmail.com

Summary

We do not collect, store, transmit, share, or sell any of your personal information. Period.

This app is a self-contained legal reference. All information shown in the app is bundled inside the app itself and shipped to your device when you download it. The app does not contact any server, does not use analytics, does not show advertising, and does not include any third-party tracking code.

What information we collect

None. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, device ID, advertising ID, or any other personal information.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not use cookies, web beacons, or local storage to track you.
  • We do not connect to our servers (we do not operate any). The app is a static reference and works completely offline.

What information we share

None. Because we do not collect any information, there is nothing to share with anyone.

We do not sell or rent data because we have no data.

Third-party services

The app does not embed or call any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. There are no Facebook, Google, or other trackers in the app.

If the app contains links to external legal-statute websites (for example, a state legislature page), tapping such a link will open your device's web browser and visit that external site. We do not control those sites and they have their own privacy policies. You can find the destination URL displayed in the app before tapping the link.

Children's privacy

This app does not collect any information from any user, including children. It is rated 17+ because it contains references to controlled substances, firearms, alcohol, tobacco, and sexual age-of-consent law — not because it collects data from minors.

Information security

Because we do not collect, transmit, or store any of your information, there is no information for a third party to intercept or access.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy (for example, if a future version of the app adds optional features that involve data collection), we will update the effective date at the top of this document and describe the change. Material changes will also be noted in the app store release notes for the version in which they take effect.

Your rights

Because we hold no information about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, delete, or export on your behalf. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at the email above.
